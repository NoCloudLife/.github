**Bring your Takeout, your 20 years of email and your unsorted photos — and leave the clouds
without losing a single byte, with undo.**

NoCloud digests your digital past — email, photo exports, bank statements, chat backups — once,
reversibly, into one normalized database you can actually query, instead of ten apps each holding
a fenced-off slice of your life. Every bulk change comes from a small, closed algebra of reversible
primitives with a full undo path; nothing is deleted without being logged first. It's local-first
by mechanism, not slogan: your data lives on hardware you own, reachable only over your own VPN.
